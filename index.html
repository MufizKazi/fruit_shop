OUR FRUITS

Mango

Mango is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, juicy flesh and distinctively rich flavor. It comes in various cultivars, with colors ranging from green to yellow to red when ripe.

Strawberry

Strawberry is a vibrant red fruit with seeds on its outer surface and a sweet, slightly tart flavor.

Water Melon

Watermelon is a large, refreshing fruit with a green rind and juicy, pink or red flesh dotted with black seeds (or seedless varieties).

Apple

Apple is a widely cultivated fruit with a crisp texture and a sweet to slightly tart taste, depending on the variety.

Pineapple

Pineapple is a tropical fruit with a rough, spiky exterior and sweet, juicy yellow flesh.

Orange

Orange is a citrus fruit with a bright, orange-colored rind and juicy, pulpy flesh segmented into slices.
